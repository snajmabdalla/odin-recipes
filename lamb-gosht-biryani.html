Lamb (Gosht) Biryani

This festive lamb biryani dish is perfect for gatherings or celebrations. Biryani is a bit of a project and is time-consuming, but I have never been disappointed with the results of this lamb biryani recipe. It's always a huge hit!

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • divided 4 small potatoes
  • peeled and halved 2 large onions

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Meanwhile, make the rice: Wash rice well and drain in a colander for at least 30 minutes
  3. Add chicken mixture and potatoes; stir gently to combine. Bring to a boil
