Vegetable Biryani

A very tasty and interesting biryani; the color looks appetizing and the taste is great! It's delicious served with mint chutney or simple, plain yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • divided 4 small potatoes
  • peeled and halved 2 large onions

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Meanwhile, make the rice: Wash rice well and drain in a colander for at least 30 minutes
  3. Add chicken mixture and potatoes; stir gently to combine. Bring to a boil
